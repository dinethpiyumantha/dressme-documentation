About DressMe

Introduction

Currently fashion design industry rapidly engaging with Information technology field. Also, shoppers now prefer online shopping than in-store shopping. But there is still a low level of user satisfaction because of the poor fit-on experience. The DressMe mobile app is developed to design customized men trousers for users. This app provides a unique service that combines the convenience of on-demand tailoring with the latest virtual reality (VR) technology. The app connects users with talented fashion designers. Measurements getting process goes with user’s uploaded images. User needs to capture front, back and side view image from mobile phone and then calculate necessary measurements from it. According to that measurement’s custom-fitted trousers are made by fashion designers. Then user can be tried on it via app’s VR feature

  • Real-time pose detection to detect two preferred postures of a person for help getting correct measurements
  • Get accurate body measurements for designing a dress
  • Human 3d modelling to create a 3d customized body model according to the measurements
  • Virtual reality to give better experience with creating virtual environment with real sized dress embedded 3d model

Objective

The DressMe mobile app's main goal is to make shopping for custom-fitted trousers simple and convenient for clients. Customers may virtually try on the trousers by utilizing the most recent VR technology, saving time and eliminating the need for several in-person fittings. DressMe also seeks to offer high quality and well-fitted pants that are customized to the customer's unique body shape by employing qualified fashion designers

Background

Traditional tailoring often involves multiple in-person fittings, which can be time-consuming and inconvenient for customers. Additionally, ready-to-wear trousers are often mass-produced and not tailored to the customer's unique body shape, leading to an ill-fitting and uncomfortable experience. Also, correctness of measurements depend on the tailors knowledge and this process is time consuming and costly. Based on the advanced development of technical devices, today’s shopping is evolving in a variety of ways, from existing offline to online. The DressMe mobile app seeks to address these issues by providing a more convenient and personalized shopping experience for men's trousers.

Research Problem

The research problem addressed by DressMe is the lack of a convenient and personalized shopping experience for men's trousers. Ready-to-wear trousers are often not tailored to the customer's unique body shape, leading to an ill-fitting and uncomfortable experience. Traditional tailoring often involves multiple in-person fittings, which can be time-consuming and inconvenient for customers.

  • No customized costume designs in online applications
  • No application to directly interact with a designer who is in another location
  • Unable to see how the costume fits actually in a virtual environment
